This proposed platform aims to:

  • Provide a framework for certification in collaboration with current educational entities

  • Make free tertiary education accessible to everyone and reduce the global cost of education

FAQ

Is this trying to substitute universities?

No, the objective is to complement them, rather than substituting them. The system will be a tool for university professors to unite efforts.

What is the role of universities in this platform?

Universities remain the primary certifying entities. The platform proposes that universities adhere to the open-source curriculum, contribute to its development, and use the content in their classes. Students' certifications are recorded on the platform, creating a standardized and compatible certification system.

How is this different from other online learning platforms?

It stands out by offering a collaborative curriculum development system and by integrating with current universities, ensuring the recognition and compatibility of the education provided.

How can people contribute to the collaborative development of the curriculum?

Everyone can create a proposal with changes to the curriculum. After a proposal is made it undergoes a voting process to decide wether it gets accepted.
