FAQ

Is this trying to substitute universities?

No, the objective is to complement them, rather than substituting them. The system will be a tool for university professors to unite efforts.

What is the role of universities then?

Universities remain the primary certifying entities. They can adhere to the open curriculum, contribute to its development and use the content in their classes. Additionally, students' certifications can be recorded on the platform, creating a standardized and compatible certification system.

How is this different from other online learning platforms?

It stands out by offering a collaborative curriculum development system and by integrating with current universities, ensuring recognition and compatibility.

How will people contribute to the collaborative development of the curriculum?

Anyone will be able create a proposal with changes to the curriculum. After a proposal is made, it will undergo a voting process to decide wether it gets accepted.
